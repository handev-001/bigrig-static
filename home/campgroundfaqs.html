Campground FAQs

What time is check-in / Check-out?
Check in at 3pm | Check out at 11am
What are the wristbands for?
The wristbands are for entry into our park, this is to monitor guest authorized to be in our park. The wristband must be affixed to the right wrist and remain on the whole time you are staying with us. Most Sturgis campgrounds require wristband for entry.
Do you have shower faclitiies?
Yes, we have showers in both the North and South parks.
Can I arrive earlier than my scheduled arrival date?
It depends if your site is available. Please call us to check availability for your site. You will be required to pay for early arrival days, regardless if you plan on leaving before your departure date. There is no refunds or credits given for early departures.
Can I get a refund if I leave early?
Refunds are not allowed for early departures.
Can I have a package shipped to me?
Yes, please send all packages to: 3333 Dolan Creek Road, Sturgis, SD 57785 in care of your name.
Can I order pizza delivery?
Yes, you can have pizza delivered from Pizza Ranch (605-347-3400) or Pizza Hut (605-347-4573).
Can I pay for extra guests when I arrive?
Yes, extra guests can be paid for upon arrival.
Can I transfer my reservation to someone else?
Yes, you can transfer your reservation to someone else but there is a transfer fee. Please see terms and conditions for details.
Can I walk to main street?
Sturgis Main Street is located 1.5 miles East of Big Rig RV Park. You can walk there but it's a bit of a hike.
Can my friends stay with me?
Yes, we allow extra guests but there is an extra guest fee of $15, plus tax, per person per night.
Can you refund my deposit?
The $95 deposit is non-refundable per the terms and conditions of the reservation contract.
Do I need a garden hose?
Yes, you need a garden hose to connect your RV to our water tap.
Do you allow ATV's?
Yes, we allow ATV's but you must obey the speed limit.
Do you allow barbeque grills?
Yes, we do allow enclosed barbeque grills in accordance with the National Forest Service. For more information about campfires in the Black Hills see the National Forest Service website.
Do you allow campfires?
No, we do not allow open campfires due to restriction of the Black Hills Forest Service. However, propane fire pits are allowed. For more information about campfires in the Black Hills see the National Forest Service Website.
Do you allow children?
We allow children as long as they are attended at at all times. However, the Sturgis downtown is not really appropriate for children.
Do you allow dry docking?
Yes. We do allow dry docking for 1 or 2 nights only but with a fee.
Do you allow firearms?
Firearms are not allowed on the property. Violation of this rule is reasonable cause for expulsion without refund.
Do you allow fireworks?
No. The Black Hills National Forest Service does not allow fireworks.
Do you allow golf carts?
Yes, we allow golf carts.
Do you allow nudity?
No, we are not that kind of campground. We cater to the more mature individuals that do not want to experience that type exposures.
Do you allow partying?
We are one of the quieter campgrounds for the Sturgis Rally. You can certainly drink alcohol and visit outside your RV, but we do not allow loud rowdy party activities. Please respect your neighbor. No loud music is allowed after 10:00 pm.
Do you allow pets?
We allow dogs as long as they are on a leash at all times. Cats are also allowed as long as they are remain in your RV.
Do you allow propane fire pits?
Yes, we do allow enclosed propane fire pits in accordance with the National Forest Service. For more information about campfires in the Black Hills see the National Forest Service website.
Do you allow side-by-sides?
Yes, we allow side-by-sides but you must obey the speed limit.
Do you allow tent camping?
We do allow tent camping on your RV site. However, Big Rig RV Park is not really well suited for tent camping because it has a gravel surface.

About Big Rig RV Park Campground

Big Rig RV Park Campground is a scenic Sturgis campground with spectacular views of the Black Hills located just 2 miles from the legendary Sturgis Main Street featuring large, full-service, pull-thru, RV campsites for the Sturgis Motorcycle Rally.
