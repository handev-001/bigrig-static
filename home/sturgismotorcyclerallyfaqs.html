Sturgis Motorcycle Rally FAQs

When is the Sturgis Motorcycle Rally?
The Sturgis Motorcycle Rally event is on the first week of August. Please see SturgisMotorcycleRally.com for more details.
How many people to go the rally?
Attendance can vary but is usually between 200,000 and 500,000 depending on the year.
What lodging is available for the rally?
There are various types of lodging including hotels and campgrounds.

About Big Rig RV Park Campground

Big Rig RV Park Campground is a scenic Sturgis campground with spectacular views of the Black Hills located just 2 miles from the legendary Sturgis Main Street featuring large, full-service, pull-thru, RV campsites for the Sturgis Motorcycle Rally.
