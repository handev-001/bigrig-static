2023 Campground Rates

Now Accepting 2023 Reservations

We have several lots available for the 2023 Sturgis Motorcycle Rally

All sites have full services (water, sewer, 30/50 amp electric) including WIFI

Reservations include two wristbands for the RV park. Extra writstbands are $18/night per person

There is a $79 non-refundable deposit that is required at the time of booking. This deposit applies towards your balance but is not refundable.


Waiting List

You can add or remove yourself from the waiting list by calling 605-347-1510 and pressing 3 at the main menu.

Shuttle Service

Starting August 4th through August 13th, 2023 from 12 noon - 2am daily, the Sturgis Da Bus Service will stop at Big Rig RV Park Campground about every 45 minutes. You can catch a ride with Da Bus to visit the legendary Sturgis Main Street or other campgrounds. Sturgis Main Street is about a 5 minute ride from our RV Park Campground. The Da Bus service is an extra fee and wristbands can be purchased here.


Availability

We still have some availability. Please check our Availabiltiy page located here for openings. Please call if are looking for two or more lots side-by-side.

Rally Schedules

We have seven (7) different rally schedules to accommodate your travel plans. The rates vary by size and location of the lot. We are open from May 1, 2023 and into the fall this year.

There are 12 different booking segments (i.e. 1a, 1b, 2a, 2b, 3a, 3b, 3c, etc) in the table below. Any segment can be booked by itself. Two segments on the same schedule can be booked together. Lot Classes

We have a variety of lot classes to accommodate your camping experience. We are well known for our beautiful view lots with stunning views of the Black Hills and the Sturgis area. All of our lots are come with full service hook-ups including water, sewer, 30/50 amp electrictiy and WiFi (see footnote 3). The lots have a defined perimeter and a gravel surface (see footnote #9).

We have two RV parking areas, our original site (North Park) constructed in 2000, and our new expansion site (South Park) completed in July, 2015 for the 75th Sturgis Rally. We recommend that you watch our YouTube video so that you can appreciate the beautiful views that we have to offer. We also have a campground map located here. Notes:

1.   Rates do not include 9.0% sales and tourism tax.

2.   A reservation includes a wristband admission for two occupants, you and your partner. Each additional occupant (above the two included) must purchase a wristband. There is a $18 charge per person per night for the wristband.

3.   We no longer offer cable television at any campsite.

4.   Reservations are transferable for a but a $100 tranfer fee applies.

5.   There are no swimming pools, hot-tubs, or saunas located on the property.

6.   We no longer offers companion sites where one RV parks backwards so your doors open towards each other.

About Big Rig RV Park Campground

Big Rig RV Park Campground is a scenic Sturgis campground with spectacular views of the Black Hills located just 2 miles from the legendary Sturgis Main Street featuring large, full-service, pull-thru, RV campsites for the Sturgis Motorcycle Rally.
