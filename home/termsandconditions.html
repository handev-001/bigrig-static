Non-Rally Cancellation Policy

Cancellations within two (2) days of arrival will result in full forfeiture of deposit. Cancellations three (3) days or more prior to arrival will result in a $10 cancellation fee.

Motorcycle Rally Cancellation Policy

Big Rig RV Park, LLC offers a 100% - no questions asked refund (minus the $79 non-refundable deposit), on rally cancellations thru March 31st, 2023. We offer a 70% - no questions asked refund (minus the $79 non-refundable deposit), on reservations cancelled between April 1st, 2023 and May 31st, 2023. Please allow 4 weeks for refund. All sales are FINAL on or after June 1st, 2023. We do not offer any refunds on or after June 1st, 2023.

Credit Card Refunds

It takes 2-7 business days to process a refund with Square. Once the refund is processed and sent to your customer’s card issuing bank, it can take another 2-7 business days (depending on the bank’s processing speeds) for the refund to post to your customer’s account. In total it can take 9-14 business days for your customer to receive their refund.

Motorcycle Rally Reservation Transferability

We understand that unexpected circumstances may change your plans. We allow the reservation for your RV site to be transferred to another individual for a fee of $100. To have the transfer process completed, please provide written notification of the request for transfer.

Limitations and Exclusions

Big Rig RV Park, LLC no longer offers cable television at any of our campsites. Big Rig RV Park, LLC does not provide onsite food, beverage, or entertainment. Big Rig RV Park no longer offers companion sites where one RV parks backwards so your doors open towards each other. Wi-Fi performance is not guaranteed. Big Rig RV Park reservations do NOT include Sturgis Bus (DaBus) Passes. For an aerial view of our facilities please watch the YouTube video.

Park Rules

  • Check-in time is after 11 a.m.
  • Check-out time is by 10 a.m.
  • No generators; please use the 20/30/50-amp service.
  • Pets must be on a leash at all times. To avoid disturbing your neighbors, please restrain your pet from aggressive behavior and barking, and collect pet droppings immediately.
  • No smoking is allowed in the shower house or park office.
  • No campfires are allowed in the park.
  • Quiet time is from 11 p.m. to 7 a.m.
  • Speed limit inside the park is 10 mph.
  • When entering and exiting the park, please do so as safely and quietly as possible.
  • Guests may visit you but must check-in at the office to receive a temporary wristband. All guests must vacate the park by 9 p.m. or purchase an overnight wristband.
  • Fireams and Fireworks are not allowed in the RV park.

About Big Rig RV Park Campground

Big Rig RV Park Campground is a scenic Sturgis campground with spectacular views of the Black Hills located just 2 miles from the legendary Sturgis Main Street featuring large, full-service, pull-thru, RV campsites for the Sturgis Motorcycle Rally.
