Big Rig RV Park Campground is one of the oldest campgrounds to offer accommodations during the Sturgis Motorcycle Rally. Big Rig RV Park Campground is located in Sturgis, South Dakota. The campground is perched on top of a hill and has spectacular views the Sturgis and the surrounding Black Hills area. We are just two miles from the legendary Sturgis Main Street with very easy access to I-90, Boulder Canyon, Vanocker Canyon, and other Black Hills attractions.

We have a variety of campsites available designed to accommodate RVs of all sizes and provide beautiful views of the surrounding Black Hills.. Our campsites feature full service hook-ups including water, sewer, 30/50 amp electricity, and WIFI that actually works. Our campsites have a gravel surface and are nearly perfectly level with little or no blocking required. We have two sections, the original North site launched in 2000, and the South site constructed in 2015. Both sections include a very clean men's and women's shower-house.

The official dates for the 2023 Sturgis Motorcycle Rally are Friday, August 4th thru Sunday, August 13th, 2023. However, many of our guests arrive early or come in later in order to avoid the crowds. We are open from May 1, 2023 and into the fall this year. We have 8 different schedules to accommodate your travel plans. We look forward to hosting you and your family this camping season or during the 2023 Sturgis Motorcycle Rally.
